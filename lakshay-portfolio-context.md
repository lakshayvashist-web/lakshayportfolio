**Practo x SSB — GMV Growth Strategy**
A 24-month GMV growth case for Practo (Practo today: ₹3,500+ Cr GMV, ₹234 Cr revenue, 6.7% take rate, 40+ Cr cumulative customers). The core diagnosis: Practo doesn't have a scale problem, it has a take-rate problem — it facilitates roughly the same GMV as Urban Company on a similar base but monetizes ~4x less per rupee transacted. The standout insight, surfaced through primary research (25 IDIs, 308 social listening posts across 11 platforms, 41 Trustpilot verbatims, 1,200+ secondary findings, and a 58-person survey), was that 67% of in-person visits leak offline before they convert into a booked, monetized Practo transaction. The recommendation was four integrated GMV plays — stop transactional leakage, D2C OPD via insurance bundling, tertiary vertical bundling (surgery), and ABHA on-ramp + chronic care subscription — projecting ₹1,075–2,400 Cr incremental GMV over 24 months on a phased roadmap (Build → Launch & Expand → Scale → Optimize & Lead).
